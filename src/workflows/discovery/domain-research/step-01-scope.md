# Step 1: Scope

## Context
Define the scope and questions for domain research.

## Instructions

### 1. Identify Domain

Clarify the domain/industry:
- What industry/market?
- What technology area?
- What geographic scope?

### 2. Define Research Questions

Create specific questions to answer:
- What do we need to know?
- What decisions depend on this research?
- What's the priority?

### 3. Set Boundaries

Define what's in/out of scope:
- Time range for data
- Geographic limitations
- Depth of research

### 4. Identify Sources

List sources to investigate:
- Industry reports
- Competitor websites
- Academic papers
- News and trends
- Community forums
