# Step 2: Research

## Context
Execute research based on defined scope and questions.

## Instructions

### 1. Gather Information

For each research question:
- Search for relevant sources
- Extract key information
- Note source credibility
- Flag conflicting information

### 2. Competitive Analysis (if applicable)

For each competitor:
- Product/features
- Pricing
- Target market
- Strengths
- Weaknesses
- Market position

### 3. Technology Evaluation (if applicable)

For each technology:
- Capabilities
- Limitations
- Community/support
- Learning curve
- Cost
- Integration options

### 4. Market Analysis (if applicable)

- Market size (TAM/SAM/SOM)
- Growth rate
- Key players
- Trends
- Barriers to entry

### 5. Validate Findings

Cross-reference sources to ensure accuracy.
