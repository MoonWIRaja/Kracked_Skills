# Step 1: Setup

## Context
You are preparing for a brainstorm session based on the product brief.

## Instructions

### 1. Load Product Brief

Read `.kracked/KD_output/product-brief/product-brief.md`:
- Understand the problem statement
- Note target users
- Note constraints and assumptions

### 2. Define Brainstorm Focus

Set the session parameters:
- **Topic:** What specific aspect to brainstorm?
- **Constraints:** What limitations exist?
- **Goals:** What defines a good idea?

### 3. Choose Technique

Select brainstorm technique based on project type:
- **SCAMPER:** Good for improving existing products
- **Mind Mapping:** Good for exploring new domains
- **Six Thinking Hats:** Good for complex decisions
- **Crazy 8s:** Good for UI/UX ideation

### 4. Set Time Box

Define time limits:
- Ideation: 15-30 minutes
- Evaluation: 10-15 minutes
- Summary: 10 minutes

## Notes
- Keep constraints realistic but don't stifle creativity
- Focus on quantity of ideas first, quality later
- Encourage wild ideas - they often lead to innovative solutions
